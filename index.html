VR Fingerprint Research

Purpose of This Research

We want to extract device-specific features from motion sensors in VR headsets. Those features can be used to do user identification, fingerprinting of devices, and behavior profiling.

You are expected to have one of Meta Quest 2, Meta Quest 3, or Meta Quest 3s. Any other types of VR headsets are not supported.

Each device is allowed to participate only once. Multiple attempts will result in rejection.

Steps to Collect Your IMU Data

  1. Please make sure your device is connected to Wi-Fi during the experiment.
  2. Please make sure your device is on a separate table without any laptops, speakers, or anything else that could cause vibrations.
  3. Please make sure your Display-off time is set to at least 15 minutes. (Library -> Settings -> General -> Power -> Display off)
  4. Enable the Developer Mode of your headset using the Meta Horizon app on your smartphone (Meta Horizon app -> Device (Upper left corner) -> Headset Settings -> Developer Mode)
  5. Download the APK: VRSensorLog.apk
  6. In the Meta Horizon Store, search and install the Mobile VR Station.
  7. Open Mobile VR Station -> Allow Permissions -> Click continue at the bottom -> Configuration Wizard -> Show All Options -> Configure Manage All Files -> Open Files All -> Locate VRSensorLog.apk -> Settings -> Allow from this source -> Install
  8. Open “AndroidSensorLog” from the Mobile VR Station on your headset.

There are 3 assignments for you to finish:

Assignment 1:
  1. Put on your headset.
  2. Press Start, take off your headset, and put it on the table.
  3. After 5 minutes, put on your headset, select Stop and Upload, and wait for Upload Successful. to show at the bottom of the screen.

Failure to upload a valid IMU data file with ≥210,000 samples will result in rejection.

Assignment 2:
  1. Put on your headset.
  2. Press Next and SIT STILL.
  3. After 5 minutes, select Stop and Upload and wait for Upload Successful. to show at the bottom of the screen.

Failure to upload a valid IMU data file with ≥210,000 samples will result in rejection.

Assignment 3:
  1. Put on your headset.
  2. Press Next and STAND STILL.
  3. After 5 minutes, select Stop and Upload and wait for Upload Successful. to show at the bottom of the screen.

Failure to upload a valid IMU data file with ≥210,000 samples will result in rejection.

A 6-digits code is generated at the bottom of the screen. Please enter this code in the corresponding section back in the website. Failure to enter a valid code will result in rejection.

Before submitting the assignments, please enter your email in the VR application. Failure to enter a valid email address will result in rejection.
